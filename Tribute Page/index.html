Mustafa Kemal ATATÜRK

The founding father of the Republic of Turkey

Here's a time line of Atatürk's life:

  • 1881 - Atatürk was born in Thessaloniki.
  • 1893 - Mustafa starts the Military Preparatory School in Thessaloniki and is given his second name "Kemal" by his teacher.
  • 1899 - Mustafa Kemal enrolls the preparation class of Military Academy in Istanbul.
  • 1905 - Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy as a Staff Captain and was sent to Damascus to serve in the fifth Army in Damascus.
  • 1915 - WW1 started, and Alliance landed troops in Gallipoli. Mustafa Kemal stopped them from advancing with his division.
  • 1916 - Mustafa Kemal is promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal leaves Istanbul and sets foot in Samsun.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal resigns from the army.
  • 1920 - Mustafa Kemal opens the Grand National Assembly of Turkey in Ankara.
  • 1923 - The Republic of Turkey is declared and Mustafa Kemâl is elected the first President.
  • 1934 - The Turkish Parliament gives Mustafa Kemal the last name "Atatürk".
  • 1938 - Passes away at the age of 57.

"There are two Mustafa Kemals; one is flesh-and-bone Mustafa Kemal who now stands before you and will pass away. The other one is you, all of you who will go to the far corners of our land to spread the ideals which must be defended with your lives if necessary. I stand for the nation's dreams, and my life's work is to make them come true."

-- Atatürk
